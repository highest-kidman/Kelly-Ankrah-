Style Tips

Expert advice to elevate your fashion game

Featured Tip

Building a Capsule Wardrobe

A capsule wardrobe is a curated collection of essential pieces that work together seamlessly. Start with neutral basics like a white button-down, well-fitted jeans, a blazer, and a little black dress. These foundational pieces can be mixed and matched to create countless outfit combinations.

Focus on quality over quantity
Stick to a cohesive color palette
Choose versatile, timeless pieces
Color Theory

Mastering Color Coordination

Understanding color theory can transform your styling game. Complementary colors create striking contrasts, while analogous colors offer harmonious, sophisticated looks.

Try monochromatic outfits for effortless elegance
Accessories

The Power of Accessories

Accessories can completely transform an outfit. A statement necklace can elevate a simple dress, while a structured bag adds sophistication to casual wear.

Follow the "one statement piece" rule
Fit & Silhouette

Dressing for Your Body

The key to looking great is understanding your body shape and choosing silhouettes that flatter your natural proportions. Confidence is your best accessory.

Tailoring makes all the difference
Seasonal

Seasonal Transitions

Master the art of transitional dressing by layering smartly. A light cardigan, scarf, or jacket can help you adapt your favorite pieces across seasons.

Invest in quality transitional pieces
Professional

Workplace Elegance

Professional doesn't mean boring. Incorporate personality through subtle details like interesting textures, quality fabrics, or a pop of color in your accessories.

Quality basics are your foundation
Trends

Incorporating Trends

Don't let trends overwhelm your personal style. Choose one or two trend elements that speak to you and integrate them with your existing wardrobe staples.

Start small with trendy accessories

Quick Style Hacks

The French Tuck

Tuck just the front of your shirt into your pants for an effortlessly chic look that defines your waist.

Layer Like a Pro

Start with fitted pieces closest to your body and add looser layers on top for depth and interest.

Shoe Game Strong

Your shoes can make or break an outfit. Invest in quality basics: nude heels, white sneakers, and black boots.

Balance Proportions

If you're wearing something loose on top, balance it with something fitted on the bottom, and vice versa.

Confidence is Key

The best accessory you can wear is confidence. Own your style choices and wear them with pride.

Tailoring Matters

A $50 dress that fits perfectly looks better than a $500 dress that doesn't. Find a good tailor.
