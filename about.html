About LUXE

Discover the story behind our passion for fashion

Our Story

Founded in 2018, LUXE Fashion began as a vision to bridge the gap between high-end luxury and accessible style. Our founders, Maria Santos and Alexandra Chen, met at fashion week in Paris and discovered their shared passion for democratizing fashion.

What started as a small boutique in downtown Manhattan has grown into a global brand that celebrates individuality while honoring timeless design principles. We believe that everyone deserves to feel confident and beautiful in what they wear.

Today, LUXE continues to evolve, constantly seeking new designers and innovative pieces that reflect the diverse, dynamic world we live in.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through fashion by providing carefully curated, high-quality pieces that celebrate personal style and promote self-expression.

Our Values

  • Quality craftsmanship in every piece
  • Sustainable and ethical fashion practices
  • Inclusive sizing and diverse representation
  • Exceptional customer service
  • Supporting emerging designers
